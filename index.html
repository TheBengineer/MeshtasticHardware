Suggested listing criteria

Open design files

Prefer projects with published schematics, PCB layouts, CAD models, firmware, or build instructions, but closed source is acceptable.

Mesh compatibility

Clearly indicate whether the hardware targets Meshtastic, MeshCore, both, or general LoRa experimentation.

Build status

Label projects as production, beta, prototype, archived, or experimental so builders know what to expect.
